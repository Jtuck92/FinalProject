How do exchanges work?


1. Sign up & get matched

Sign up for the exchange.
We will close signups and our computer program will choose who you should send a gift to (this is the most important part; this is about the giving!).

2. Find the perfect gift & send it

Once you come back here to pick up your match's information, ship your gift.
When you ship it, keep a receipt and come back here to tell us you've shipped it.

3. Receive a gift from your match

When you receive a gift, you'll need to come back here and post an amazing thank you note, hopefully with photos!
