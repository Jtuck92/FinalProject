About Giftr

Our mission is to connect people around the world and spread joy. We wanted individuals to feel good about sending and recieving gifts year round. What started off as a Secret Santa Giftr exchange has turned into a community of indivudals that love seeing the joy that others have brought them. Oh, and we also did this for our Final Project for Skill Distillery Cohort 27.
